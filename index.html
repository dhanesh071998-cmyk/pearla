When you use Pearla, we collect:

  • Account data: Your name and email address (required to create an account)

  • Usage data: Decision count, timestamps, and plan status

2. How We Store It

  • Firebase Firestore (Google): Stores your account profile, decisions, and usage quota
  • Cloudinary: Stores images you upload for visual decisions
  • All data is stored securely with industry-standard encryption

3. How We Use It

  • To generate personalised AI decisions using Google Gemini AI
  • To enforce your daily free decision quota (3/day)
  • To show you your decision history and behavioural patterns
  • To process payments (Razorpay for India, Stripe for global users)

  • Google Gemini AI: Your decision text and images are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate responses. Google's privacy policy applies to this processing.
  • Firebase (Google): Database and authentication provider
  • Cloudinary: Image hosting provider
  • Razorpay / Stripe: Payment processors. We do not store card details.

  • Export your decision history
